# Fable-Style Demo Recording Brief

Purpose: make a short product story that can stand in for live clicking or support outreach. Keep it calm and source-led, not hype-led.

## Format

- Length: 60-75 seconds.
- Surface: `/showcase` first, `/demo` for atmosphere if needed.
- Ratio: 16:9 for pitch; export a square crop later only if outreach needs it.
- Cursor: visible but slow. No frantic clicking.
- Voice: founder walkthrough, not advert.

## Story Arc

1. **Before:** dense tender page.
2. **Read:** Bidframe turns it into requirements.
3. **Risk:** deal-breakers rise to the top.
4. **Proof:** click one, show source page and clause.
5. **Answer:** evidence-backed draft appears.
6. **Control:** confirm-before-approve, edit, flag.
7. **Honesty:** open question instead of invented answer.
8. **Close:** "Bring us one tender. We will read it with you."

## Script

"This is a real public-sector tender. In one clause, there are five ways to get the bid thrown out. A bid manager has to find those by hand before they can safely write anything."

"Bidframe does that first read. It turns the pack into a compliance matrix, with deal-breakers pinned first."

"Every row links back to the source. Here is the insurance requirement, here is the exact clause, and here is the answer drafted from evidence we can cite."

"But the product does not decide for you. A deal-breaker needs explicit confirmation. Drafts can be edited. Rows can be flagged. And where Bidframe cannot prove an answer, it asks instead of guessing."

"That is the product: faster tender reading, with the expert still at the wheel."

## Caption Lines

- "Deal-breakers first."
- "Every line back to source."
- "Drafts only from evidence."
- "Asks instead of guessing."
- "Human in control."

## Do Not Show

- A live upload.
- API keys or dev terminals.
- Any claim not in `demo-claim-ledger.md`.
- Long raw accuracy percentages.
