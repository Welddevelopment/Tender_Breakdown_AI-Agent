## Outreach drafts (tailored — adapt per target)

**LinkedIn first-touch — consultancy founder/bid director:**
> Hi [Name] — I've built a tool that breaks a public-sector tender into a verified requirements
> checklist, flags the mandatory pass/fail ones that would disqualify a bid, and links every requirement
> back to the exact clause so you can check it in one click. Given [Firm] runs so many bids, I'd love 10
> minutes to show you on a tender you know and get your honest read on whether it'd save your team time —
> no pitch, I genuinely want an expert's take.

**LinkedIn first-touch — health & social care specialist (BFT/Della Pip/Tenders UK):**
> Hi [Name] — I know [Firm] lives in social care frameworks. I've built a tool that reads a tender and
> pulls out every requirement with its source clause, and shouts the pass/fail gates that sink a bid if
> missed. Could I show you on a care framework ITT you've worked and get your honest take? 10 mins, no sell.

**Follow-up (after a yes):**
> Brilliant, thank you. Two ways: I run it live on a past tender you pick (most fun — you'll watch it
> catch things on a doc you already know), or I send a 90-sec screen recording first. Which suits? If
> you've a tender PDF handy, even better — we'll dissect it together.

**The ask ladder (label honestly):** floor = "I'd use this" · middle = "send it, I'll run my next bid
through it" · ceiling = a tiny paid pilot (even £50–100 = real money changing hands).
